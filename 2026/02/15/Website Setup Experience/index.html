架設網站心得

我過去一直以為Blog是甚麼老人才會用的撥接式網路那個年代的產物，結果我後來發現這東西其實蠻流行的。
之前也有嘗試問AI去做但每次都不知道為甚麼都會報錯，身邊也都沒有很熟的人有做過Blog，後來就不了了之。
一直到參加 SCISTxSCAICT 2026 聯合寒訓 之後認識了WalNut，我們兩個住同一間房間，後來我看到他也有做Blog，然後我就問他能不能也幫我搞一個，想不到跟他盧一下下他就答應了。

他幫我把前面搞資料夾的工作搞好了，我只需要把一些數據改一改然後推到Github上就行了
大概過了兩三天，我的Blog終於上線了！也就是你現在看到的這個網站！
這幾天感謝很多人幫忙，中途還出了一點小插曲…

小插曲

正常流程是先跑 hexo clean hexo g hexo s 甚麼的，結果在跑測試的時候不小心搞到整個爆掉。
本來想說去把Github上正常版本的抓回來回朔，然後我不小心跑成 hexo d !!!!!!
後來要去找以前的版本，然後不小心在後台把它刪了……
我真的是差一點爆炸，最後你猜怎麼救回來的?



CTRL+Z



好啦至少救回來了。
大概就是這樣啦~

Website Setup Experience

I used to think blogs were some ancient relic from the dial-up era that only old people used, but I later realized they’re actually still pretty popular.
I tried asking AI to help me build one before, but for some reason, it kept throwing errors. Since I didn’t know anyone personally who had experience with blogging, I eventually just gave up.
That was until I attended SCISTxSCAICT 2026 United Winter Camp and met WalNut. We were roommates, and when I saw he had his own blog, I asked if he could help me set one up. Surprisingly, after a little bit of pestering, he agreed!

He handled all the initial folder setup for me, so all I had to do was tweak some data and push it to GitHub.
After about two or three days, my blog is finally live! Which is exactly what you’re looking at right now!
I’m really grateful to everyone who helped me out these past few days, even though there was a bit of a hiccup along the way…

The Hiccup
The normal workflow is to run hexo clean, hexo g, hexo s and so on, but while I was running tests, I accidentally broke the whole thing.
I thought about pulling the working version back from GitHub to revert the changes, but then I accidentally ran hexo d !!!!!!
Then, while trying to find the previous version in the backend, I accidentally deleted it…
I was honestly on the verge of a meltdown. In the end, guess how I saved it?



CTRL+Z



Well, at least I got it back.
And that’s pretty much the story!
