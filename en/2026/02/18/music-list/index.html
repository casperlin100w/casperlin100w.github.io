Curated Website Playlist

Preface: This website features a music player and a Music Page containing a collection of songs I’ve personally selected. This post is where I share the stories and reasons behind these choices.

Now, let’s dive in!

Happiness Is Singing

Happiness Is Singing

Music Info
Vocals: A-Lin
Lyrics: Hsia-Hsien Lin
Composer: Fang-Rui Li
Arrangement: Blackie / Yu-Fen Ma

This song is from the movie Sunny Girl Chorus, the highest-grossing film in Taiwan’s local cinema history.
I contributed to that box office record with a ticket of my own, and I cried my eyes out during the screening.
It’s truly a masterpiece and a real tear-jerker! Listening to it again lets me relive those emotions.
Including this song in the playlist was a must.
Fun fact: Many of the slogans across this entire site are actually lyrics from Sunny Girl Chorus…

The Song of Laughter and Forgetting

The Song of Laughter and Forgetting

Music Info
Vocals: Mayday
Performance: Mayday
Lyrics: Ashin
Composer: Monster

As a “Mayday fan” (Wu-Mi) for more years than I can count, I’ve heard this song used often during New Year’s Eve countdowns.
It uses a cheerful melody to brush past sadness and grief, allowing those feelings to be forgotten.
All that remains is this “Song of Laughter and Forgetting,” a way to bid a proper farewell to the past.
Every year when I hear this song—countdown or not—I take a moment to carefully reminisce about the good times.
Recommended Version: Fly To 2021 New Year’s Eve Online Concert

The Most Important Trifles

The Most Important Trifles

Music Info
Vocals: Mayday
Performance: Mayday
Lyrics: Ashin
Composer: Masa

My strongest memory of this song comes from the Noah’s Ark 10th Anniversary Special Online Concert; I hadn’t listened to it much before then.
The rearranged version is absolutely incredible—not that the original isn’t good or anything.
Recommended Version: Mayday Online New Year’s Eve Concert: Noah’s Ark 10th Anniversary Special Edition

Die For You

Die For You

Music Info
Lyrics: Grabbitz
Composer: Grabbitz
Vocals: Grabbitz

This song is from the 2021 VALORANT Champions. Back then, I didn’t even know what VALORANT was.
It wasn’t until I watched my first Champions tournament in 2024 that I looked this up.
Every year, the VALORANT Champions theme songs are top-tier.
This one is a classic among classics, so it deserves a special spot.

SUPERPOWER

SUPERPOWER

Music Info
Songwriting: Kill Dave, Cal Scruby, Dabin, Dalton Diehl, Gianni Taylor, Joshua Landry
Vocals: JULIE & NATTY of KISS OF LIFE, Mark Tuan

Like “Die For You,” this is a VALORANT Champions theme. This specific track accompanied the first global championship I experienced after joining the VALORANT community in early 2024.
It was such a shame that GEN.G, whom I was rooting for, didn’t take the title (just saying).

Bad Guy

Bad Guy

Music Info
Songwriting: Billie Eilish & FINNEAS
Vocals: Billie Eilish

I first heard this song in the game Just Dance 2021. The sudden shift in musical style makes it hard to believe it’s the same song.

Translated By Gemini.
